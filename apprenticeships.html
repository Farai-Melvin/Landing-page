Apprenticeships

This is by far my best bet. For me specifically, a software development one would be ideal, and they're not that difficult to find. Problem is their dates. This is May 2025, for a 70% chance at getting one I'd have to pre-register for 2026. This is absolutely fine by me. Another great thing is that as an immigrant I can find one's that offer CoSs. I've already pre-registered for one that does such. Not really a huge company I believe, but given that I impress them at the internship in May, 2026, I can get an apprenticeship for September 2026 and after 18 or 24 months they can offer me a job. If they do offer me a job, a perk would be a visa. The site says it can even be an ILR one, but I'm not raising my hopes since I'll be competing with natives who're fresh out of school and have attended multiple bootcamps whilst I'm here teaching myself all this.
